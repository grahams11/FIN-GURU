Elite Approaches to Predicting Stock/Options Price Movement
Predicting stock or options price movements (up for calls, down for puts) with high confidence is inherently probabilistic—no method guarantees success, as markets are influenced by unpredictable events like geopolitics or black swans. However, the "most elite" strategies used by hedge funds and proprietary traders combine unusual options activity (UOA) as a leading signal (spotting "smart money" flows), technical indicators for confirmation, and sentiment/news overlays for context. This beats relying on single indicators, as it captures institutional intent early.
UOA is particularly powerful for options: It flags contracts with volume-to-open-interest ratios >5x average, often signaling insider-like bets on direction. Tools like Unusual Whales or Barchart scanners highlight these in real-time. For direction: Sweeps of calls (large, multi-exchange buys) suggest upside; puts for downside.
